On the Subject of Shiro’s Birthday

When you have so many birthdays you must know how to organize the best parties.

  • Count all your birthday hats, make sure there is enough for every Walter or they will start wrecking the place.
  • There is cake! It’s not a lie. Every group of Walters will eat a cake so make sure there is enough cake for them all or they might eat everything at the house.
  • The more the merrier, count every happy Walter attending the party! You can also count unhappy Walters.
  • With all this information you can get a party meter! If the party meter is at least 100% do not stop the party because it’s your birthday for the entire bomb’s duration.
Birthday hat 20%
Birthday cake 20%
Walter 10%
Group of Walters 30%
  • For a party meter of less than 30% it’s a horrible party and the minutes drag on. You must watch the second number of the timer.
  • If it’s more than 30% then it’s a pretty good party but the seconds fly by. You must watch the third number of the timer.
  • And most importantly, you MUST count strikes!
  • Subtract unhappy types of attendees from the meter. Groups of Walters might not be satisfied with a lack of cakes but single Walters with enough hats are still happy.
